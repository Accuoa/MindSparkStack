FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need to be a customer to become an affiliate?
No, but it helps. Affiliates who've taken the course create more authentic recommendations and tend to convert better.
When and how do I get paid?
Commissions are paid monthly, with a 30-day holding period to account for refunds. We pay via PayPal or bank transfer — minimum payout is $50.
Can I promote MindSparkAI using paid ads?
Yes, with one rule: you cannot bid on branded terms like "MindSparkAI" or "MindSpark AI" in search ads. All other paid promotion is welcome.
Is there a minimum number of sales required?
No minimums. Whether you refer 1 sale or 100, you earn commission on every one.
What if someone requests a refund?
If a referred customer requests a refund within the 30-day guarantee period, the commission for that sale is reversed.
