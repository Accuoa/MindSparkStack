## Content Creation Flow

```
1. generate_content.py → produces raw content per platform
2. repurpose_content.py → 1 long piece → tweets + LinkedIn + TikTok scripts + email
3. content_scheduler.py → reads queue → dispatches to tools/Zapier
4. Archive → moves posted content to .tmp/content_archive/
```

## Platform-Specific Rules

### Twitter/X (@AccuoaAgent)
- **Character limit:** 280 per tweet
- **Threads:** 4-7 tweets, hook first, CTA last
- **Hashtags:** Max 2 per tweet (#AI, #productivity)
- **Rate limit:** 1,500 tweets/month on free tier = ~50/day
- **Engagement:** Like/reply to mentions (manual for now)

### YouTube (@Accuoa)
- **Long-form:** 8-12 min, uploaded Wednesday
- **Shorts:** 30-60 sec, #Shorts in title, 4-6/week
- **Quota:** 10,000 units/day, upload = 1,600 units (max 6 uploads/day)
- **Description:** Always include mindsparkstack.com link
- **Tags:** 5-10 relevant tags per video

### TikTok (@accuoa)
- **Video length:** 30-60 seconds
- **Style:** Fast-paced, hook in first 2 seconds
- **Posting:** MANUAL from phone (API restricted)
- **Videos generated by:** `generate_heygen_video.py --aspect 9:16`
- **Captions:** Short, punchy, 3-5 hashtags

### LinkedIn (Personal Profile)
- **Post length:** 150-300 words
- **Tone:** Professional, opinionated, no corporate speak
- **Format:** Short paragraphs, line breaks, end with question
- **Posting via:** Zapier webhook (ZAPIER_LINKEDIN_WEBHOOK in .env)

### Email Newsletter
- **Frequency:** Weekly (Friday 10AM)
- **Subject:** Under 50 characters
- **Body:** 200-400 words, personal tone from Aiden
- **List:** Supabase profiles table (purchased users) + Aiden fallback

### Blog
- **Frequency:** Weekly (Friday)
- **Format:** HTML, SEO-optimized
- **Deploy:** `deploy_to_turbify.py` to mindsparkstack.com/blog/

## Content Approval Rules

**Auto-approved (no human review):**
- Tweets and threads from content pillars
- LinkedIn posts under 300 words
- Short-form video scripts under 60 sec
- Repurposed content from approved long-form
- Blog posts generated from approved content

**Human review required:**
- Long-form YouTube videos (over 2 minutes)
- Content mentioning competitors by name
- Pricing-related content
- Content responding to controversy
- First 10 posts on any new platform

---

## Error Handling

1. If a post fails, retry up to 3 times (built into all tools)
2. After 3 failures, `tools/alert.py` sends email to Aiden
3. Failed content stays in queue (not archived)
4. Check `docs/ERROR_LOG.md` for error history
5. Rate limit errors: wait 60 seconds, then retry
