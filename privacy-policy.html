Privacy Policy

\n

Last updated: April 4, 2026  |  Effective: April 4, 2026

\n

MindSparkAI ("we," "our," or "us") operates mindsparkstack.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit our website or purchase our courses.

\n

1. Information We Collect

\n

Information you provide directly:

\n
  • Name and email address when you sign up for our free toolkit or newsletter
  • Payment information processed securely by Stripe (we never see or store full card numbers)
  • Name, email, and any messages when you contact us
\n

Information collected automatically:

\n
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages viewed, time spent, and referring URLs (via analytics)
  • IP address (anonymized where possible)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 7)
\n

2. How We Use Your Information

\n
  • To deliver the course content and materials you purchased
  • To send you the free AI Starter Toolkit you requested
  • To send course updates, newsletters, and educational content (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To process payments and manage your account
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To improve our website and course content
  • To comply with legal obligations
\n

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

\n

For users in the European Economic Area, we process your data under the following legal bases:

\n
  • Contract: Processing necessary to deliver your purchase
  • Consent: Email marketing (you can withdraw at any time)
  • Legitimate interests: Website analytics and fraud prevention
  • Legal obligation: Tax and financial record-keeping
\n

4. Data Sharing

\n

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with:

\n
  • Stripe: Payment processing (Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • Email service providers: To send transactional and marketing emails
  • Analytics providers: Aggregate, anonymized usage data
  • Legal authorities: When required by law
\n

5. Data Retention

\n

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. Purchase records are retained for 7 years for tax purposes. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time (subject to legal retention requirements).

\n

6. Your Rights

\n

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your data, or to object to processing. Email aiden@mindsparkstack.com to exercise any of these rights.

\n

7. Cookies

\n

We use essential cookies (required for site function), analytics cookies (anonymous usage data), and preference cookies (e.g., countdown timer state). You can control cookies through your browser settings.

\n

8. Security

\n

We use HTTPS encryption, secure payment processing via Stripe, and restricted access to personal data. While we take reasonable precautions, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

\n

9. Children's Privacy

\n

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

\n

10. Changes to This Policy

\n

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or website notice.
